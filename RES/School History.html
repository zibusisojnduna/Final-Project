School Histroy

In 1981 Mirriam Qgubule became the first principal in the present existing buildings,

but the school was small at that time.

She initiated in-School Training for teachers to study for a Diploma in Specialized Education through Ex-Department.

In 1991 Mrs Gqubule was transfered to St Martins Special School. The enrolement was 200 hundred at that time.

She worked hard for a number of new developments at EKukhanyeni Special School for the benefits of learners with special Education needs.

Ms M.M. Mthembu retired on 30th of June 2016. The enrolment was 360 at the time. After Ms M.M. Mthembu`s retirement, Mr H.B. Madlala who was the Deputy principal was appointed in the principal position on the 1st of December 2016.

Currently the school has four hundred (400) learners with (High Level of Support) severly intellectual chalenged. Twenty one (21) of the learners are autistic children.
